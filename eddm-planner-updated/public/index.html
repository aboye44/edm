EDDM Size Requirements

To qualify for EDDM postage rates, your mailpiece must meet these USPS size requirements:

Minimum Size (must meet at least one):

  • Length greater than 10.5"
  • Height greater than 6.125"
  • Thickness greater than 0.25"

Common EDDM Postcard Sizes:

  • 6.125" x 11" (minimum qualifying size)
  • 6.5" x 9"
  • 8.5" x 11"
  • 9" x 12"
  • 11" x 17" (folded to 11" x 8.5")
  • 12" x 15" (maximum size)

Weight Limit: 3.3 oz maximum

What is EDDM?

EDDM (Every Door Direct Mail) is a USPS program that lets businesses send mail to every address on a postal carrier route-without needing a mailing list. You select the neighborhoods, we deliver to every door.

EDDM is ideal for:

  • Local businesses targeting nearby neighborhoods
  • Grand openings and new location announcements
  • Restaurants, salons, and service businesses
  • Political campaigns and local elections
  • Real estate agents farming neighborhoods
  • Home service companies (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, landscaping)

EDDM is NOT ideal for:

  • Targeted campaigns to specific people (use Marketing Mail instead)
  • National campaigns (EDDM is designed for local saturation)
  • Small quantities under 200 pieces

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EDDM tool?

An EDDM tool helps you plan Every Door Direct Mail campaigns by visualizing carrier routes on a map, showing household counts, and calculating postage costs. Our free EDDM tool also provides instant printing quotes.

How much does EDDM cost per piece?

EDDM postage is $0.26 per piece. With printing included, expect to pay $0.35-0.50 per piece depending on size and quantity.

What is the minimum quantity for EDDM?

EDDM requires a minimum of 200 pieces per carrier route for retail mailings, or 200 pieces total for BMEU mailings.

Do I need a mailing list for EDDM?

No. That's the beauty of EDDM-you select carrier routes and USPS delivers to every address on those routes. No list required.

How long does EDDM delivery take?

EDDM typically delivers within 3-14 business days after postal drop-off, depending on USPS processing.

Can I target specific demographics with EDDM?

Yes. Our EDDM tool lets you filter routes by household income, age, and household size. You can also choose residential-only, business-only, or all addresses.
